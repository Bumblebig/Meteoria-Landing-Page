About Meteoria Weather Forecast

It all began with a fascination for the ever-changing skies, the dance of raindrops on windowpanes, and the unpredictability of nature's moods. Meteoria was born out of a genuine passion for understanding and predicting weather conditions. It's not just a project because Holberton required it; it's a project fueled by a lifelong curiosity and love for meteorology.

As a team of weather enthusiasts, we embarked on this journey to create a weather forecasting tool that's not only accurate but also easy to use. We wanted to provide a platform where anyone, anywhere, could access up-to-date weather information effortlessly.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
